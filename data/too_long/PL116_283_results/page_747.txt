SEC. 1742. DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE CYBER HYGIENE AND CYBERSECURITY MATURITY MODEL CERTIFICATION FRAMEWORK.

(a) CYBER SECURITY PRACTICES AND CAPABILITIES IN THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE.—
