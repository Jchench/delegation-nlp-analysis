''(C) CERTAIN TRANSFERS NOT AVOIDABLE.-
''(i) IN GENERAL.-Notwithstanding paragraph (11), the Board, whether acting as such or as conservator or liquidating agent of an insured credit union, may not avoid any transfer of money or other property in connection with any qualified financial contract with an insured credit union.
''(ii) EXCEPTION FOR CERTAIN TRANSFERS.-Clause (i) shall not apply to any transfer of money or other property in connection with any qualified financial contract with an insured credit union if the Board determines that the transferee had actual intent to hinder, delay, or defraud such credit union, the creditors of such credit union, or any conservator or liquidating agent appointed for such credit union.
