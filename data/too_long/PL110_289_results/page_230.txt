            (b) shall apply to buildings with respect to which housing 
            credit dollar amounts are allocated after the date of the 
            enactment of this Act.
            (B) BUILDINGS NOT SUBJECT TO ALLOCATION LIMITS.—
            To the extent paragraph (1) of section 42(h) of the Internal 
            Revenue Code of 1986 does not apply to any building by 
            reason of paragraph (4) thereof, the amendments made 
            by subsection (b) shall apply buildings financed with bonds 
            issued pursuant to allocations made after the date of the 
            enactment of this Act.
