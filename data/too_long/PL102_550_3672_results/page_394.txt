(a) SECTION 1956.--Section 1956(c) of title 18, United States Code, is amended--
   (1) in paragraph (4)(A)--
      (A) by inserting "or (iii) involving the transfer of title to any real property, vehicle, vessel, or aircraft," after "monetary instruments,"
