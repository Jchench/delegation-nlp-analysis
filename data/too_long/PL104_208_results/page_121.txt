OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE, AIR FORCE
For an additional amount for “Operation and Maintenance, Air Force”, $67,400,000, to remain available until September 30, 1997: Provided, That such amount is designated by Congress as an emergency requirement pursuant to section 251(b)(2)(D)(i) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985, as amended: Provided further, That these funds may be used to liquidate obligations incurred by the Air Force during fiscal year 1996 for costs incurred under the authority of the Feed and Forage Act (41 U.S.C. 11).
