SEC. 103. BUDGET CROSSCUT.

The Office of Management and Budget is directed to submit to the House and Senate Committees on Appropriations, as part of the President’s Fiscal Year 1998 Budget, an interagency budget crosscut that displays Federal spending for fiscal years 1993 through 1998 on ecosystem restoration and other purposes in the Bay-Delta region, separately showing funding provided previously or requested under both preexisting authorities and new authorities granted by this title.

SEC. 104. EFFECTIVE DATE.

Section 102 of this title shall take effect on the date of passage of California State Proposition 204.

This Act may be cited as the “Omnibus Consolidated Appropriations Act, 1997”.
