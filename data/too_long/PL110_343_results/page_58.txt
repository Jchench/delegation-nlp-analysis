    Subtitle B—Carbon Mitigation and Coal Provisions
    
    SEC. 111. EXPANSION AND MODIFICATION OF ADVANCED COAL PROJECT INVESTMENT CREDIT.
    
    Section 48A(a) of the (a) MODIFICATION OF CREDIT AMOUNT.—code is amended by striking ‘‘and’’ at the end of paragraph (1), by striking the period at the end of paragraph (2) and inserting ‘‘, and’’, and by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
            ‘‘(3) 30 percent of the qualified investment for such taxable year in the case of projects described in clause (iii) of subsection (d)(3)(B).’’.

    (b) EXPANSION OF AGGREGATE CREDITS.—Section 48A(d)(3)(B) is amended by striking ‘‘$1,300,000,000’’ and inserting ‘‘$2,550,000,000’’.
