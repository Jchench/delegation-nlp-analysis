SEC. 1087. AMENDMENTS TO THE FAIR CREDIT BILLING ACT.
    The Fair Credit Billing Act (15 U.S.C. 1666—1666j) is amended

section 105(i) (as added by this subtitle) and inserting “Bureau”.
